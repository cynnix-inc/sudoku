How to play

Goal: Fill the grid so each row, column, and 3×3 box contains 1–9 with no repeats.

Basics

  • Click a cell and type 1–9, or use the number pad.
  • Erase with Delete/Backspace or Erase button.
  • Use Notes (✎) to place small candidates.

Shortcuts

  • Undo/Redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y
  • Arrows: move selection
  • 0/Delete: erase
