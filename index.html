How to play

Goal: Fill the grid so each row, column, and 3×3 box contains 1–9 with no repeats.

❤️ Lives

Lives are the number of wrong entries allowed. Each unique incorrect number placed in a cell uses one life. When you run out of lives, the game ends.

You can change Lives in Settings. Set to 11 for Unlimited. Daily puzzles use fixed Lives based on difficulty, so your setting won’t override Daily.

💡 Hints

Hints help in different ways depending on the selected Hint type. Hints are limited by difficulty (Easy 5, Medium 3, Hard 2, Expert 1, Master/Extreme 0). Each hint adds +30s to your timer and marks the run as assisted (assisted runs don’t update your personal best).

Hint types
  • Direct number placementFills one correct digit into a random empty cell. Quick progress without revealing the logic.
  • Logic‑based guidanceHighlights one cell that can be solved logically, shows its candidates, and gives a brief reason (e.g., “Naked single”, “Hidden single”). You place the digit yourself.
  • Highlight candidates / safe numbersShows pencil‑mark candidates for empty cells and highlights a promising cell (often with the fewest candidates). Keeps you in control while reducing mistakes.
  • Escalating assistanceTiered help on repeated taps: (1) highlight the next solvable cell → (2) show/narrow candidates → (3) reveal the answer. Cycles through as needed.

Change Hint type in Settings → Gameplay → Hint type. The time penalty applies to every hint action, regardless of type.

🔄 Sync

  • Signed‑in syncWhen signed in, your stats and gameplay/calendar settings sync across devices.
  • Device‑specificAppearance choices (dark mode and accent color) stay on each device.
  • OfflineIf you’re not signed in, everything is kept locally in your browser.

🧘 Zen mode

  • FocusHides the on‑screen timer, Lives, difficulty chip, and status messages.
  • LivesNormal games use Unlimited Lives in Zen. Daily puzzles keep their fixed Lives.
  • StatsZen runs don’t update wins/losses or best times.
  • ToggleTurn on/off in Settings. It takes effect immediately without interrupting your game.
