## Easter Eggs Guide

A quick reference for hidden treats in the Ultimate Sudoku app: how to trigger each and what they do.

### Konami Retro Mode
- **Trigger**: Press Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A (keyboard).
- **Effect**: Enables a retro CRT-style theme for the session.
- **Notes**: One-way enable; reload to clear.

### Triple‑click Logo Surprise
- **Trigger**: Triple‑click the `Ultimate Sudoku` title in the header.
- **Effect**: Pixel confetti burst near the logo and a random accent color is applied.

### Seeded Puzzle Loader
- **Trigger**: Hold Alt and click `New Game`, type a seed, then press Enter.
- **Effect**: Starts a deterministic puzzle generated from your seed at the current difficulty.
- **Notes**: Starting a seeded game while a game is in progress prompts to end the current run.

### Neon Trail for Speedy Wins
- **Trigger**: Win a puzzle with a finish time under 3:00.
- **Effect**: The grid gets a brief neon hover trail effect for 10 seconds on the win screen.

### Seasonal Themes
- **Trigger**: Date‑based; auto‑applies on load.
  - Mar 14 (Pi Day): Adds π‑flavored UI flair.
  - May 4: Adds a subtle “lightsaber” highlight.

### Palindrome Wink
- **Trigger**: Any row becomes exactly `123456789` in order.
- **Effect**: Shows a small “Nice ordering.” toast.
- **Notes**: Shown once per row.

### Rapid Navigation Glow
- **Trigger**: Navigate cells rapidly (≥10 directional key presses in 2 seconds). Arrows, WASD, or HJKL count.
- **Effect**: Board gets a soft glow for ~5 seconds.

### Wiggle on Win (and Confetti Rules)
- **Trigger**: Place the final correct number (puzzle solved).
- **Effect**: The board does a tiny celebratory wiggle.
- **Confetti**: Shown on the win banner only if no hints were used (unassisted run).

### Rainbow Digits (Next Game)
- **Trigger**: Finish a puzzle with no mistakes and no hints used (perfect run).
- **Effect**: Number pad digits show rainbow tints on your next game.
- **Notes**: Applies once; automatically clears after the next game loads.
